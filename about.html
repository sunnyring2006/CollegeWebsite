Our Legacy

75+ Years of Engineering Excellence

A Rich History

Established in 1947, Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli, is one of the oldest and most premier engineering institutions in India. Named after the industrialist Seth Walchand Hirachand, the college has been a pioneer in technical education.

  • Autonomous Institute since 2007
  • Govt. Aided Institution
  • NBA Accredited Programs

To produce capable graduate engineers with an aptitude for research and leadership.
